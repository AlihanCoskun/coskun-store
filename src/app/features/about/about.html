About Us

Sevilcandan — the purest, calmest expression of healing.

Born from the vision of Sevilcandan — a nurse who embraced healing not just as a profession but as a way of being — our brand is inspired by the graceful gifts of nature.

Our purpose is to bring back those moments of calm that modern life so easily makes us forget. Our soaps and candles are designed to transform everyday rituals into gentle wellbeing experiences.

Each formula is crafted with pure ingredients, natural essences and carefully considered textures — so that every lather and every scent brings a quiet sense of balance and freshness into your space.
